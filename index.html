FEMA Disaster Declarations

Interactive visualizations comparing major disaster declaration outcomes across seven presidential administrations (2001–present).
Each chart is interactive — hover for details, click legends to filter, and download as PNG.
  • 1 Summary Table
    Side-by-side comparison of approvals, denials, denial rates, and median processing times for all seven administrations.
  • 2 Approval & Denial Rates
    Stacked bar chart showing the proportion of approved vs. denied disaster requests by administration.
  • 3 Processing Time
    Median and average days from request to decision for both approvals and denials.
  • 4 Per-Year Rates
    Annualized approval and denial counts, normalizing for differences in term length.
  • 5 Yearly Trend
    Year-by-year timeline of approved and denied declarations from 2001 to present, with administration bands and transition markers.
  • 6 Disaster Types
    Grouped bar chart breaking down declarations by incident type (hurricanes, severe storms, fires, etc.) across administrations.
  • 7 Political Context Scatter Plot
    Butterfly scatter plot showing disaster outcomes vs. state vote margin, revealing whether political alignment affects declaration decisions.
Data source: OpenFEMA API — FemaWebDisasterDeclarations & DeclarationDenials datasets.
Vote margins from official state election results (2000–2024). COVID-19 (Biological) declarations excluded.
