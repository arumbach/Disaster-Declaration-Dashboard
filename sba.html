The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) provides low-interest disaster loans to homeowners, renters, businesses, and nonprofits affected by declared disasters. This project analyzes SBA disaster loan data across multiple fiscal years, examining approved loan amounts, verified losses, and geographic patterns in disaster lending activity.

Disclaimer: These analyses are based on data from the SBA Open Data Portal and are works-in-progress. Dollar values represent original verified loss and approved loan amounts. An optional inflation adjustment normalizes values to January 2026 dollars using CPI-U data from the Bureau of Labor Statistics. If you have questions or find an error, please contact me.
