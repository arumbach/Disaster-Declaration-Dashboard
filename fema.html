Live-updating dashboard tracking disaster declaration requests, approvals, denials, and processing times since January 20, 2025.

Butterfly scatter plot showing whether a state's political alignment affects disaster declaration decisions, across seven administrations.

Side-by-side comparison of approvals, denials, denial rates, and median processing times for all seven administrations (2001-present).

Major Disaster Declaration Denial Rate by Administration
Stacked bar chart showing the proportion of approved vs. denied disaster requests by administration.
 Visualization
Days from Declaration Request to Decision by Administration
Median and average days from disaster declaration request to decision for both approvals and denials.
 Visualization
Major Disaster Declarations and Denials by Year
Year-by-year timeline of approved and denied declarations from 2001 to present, with administration bands and transition markers.

Year-by-year median processing times for approvals and denials, showing how quickly disaster declarations are decided over time.
Data source: OpenFEMA API — FemaWebDisasterDeclarations & DeclarationDenials datasets. Vote margins from official state election results (2000-2024). COVID-19 (Biological) declarations excluded.
